Privacy Notice

Rural Funds Management Limited (ACN 077 492 838, AFSL 226 701) (RFM) and its related entities recognise that your privacy is important and we are committed to ensuring the confidentiality and security of personal information we collect.
‍
In handling this information, RFM is bound by the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth), the Australian Privacy Principles, and RFM's Privacy Notice. This notice sets out how we collect, use, disclose and otherwise manage your personal information.
‍
RFM is committed not to use or disclose personal information that it has collected, other than in the manner defined within the Australian Privacy Principles.

If you would like more information about how RFM handles your information, please view our Privacy Notice.
