Privacy policy
Protecting your privacy is very important to Glencore Australia Holdings Pty Ltd, its Australian related bodies corporate and associated entities ("Glencore", "our", "us" or "we").
We are committed to complying with the Australian Privacy Principles contained in the
Privacy Act 1988 (Cth)
("Privacy Act") which require us to have an up-to-date policy about how we manage Personal Information. Our Privacy Policy and Privacy Collection Statements detail how we collect, use and manage Personal Information depending on the type of relationship you have with us. Our Privacy Policy and Privacy Collection Statements are available below.
Please refer to the
Glencore Group Privacy Notice
for information regarding the processing of personal data by the Glencore Group.
