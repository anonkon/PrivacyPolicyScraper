More advice and support



Want to learn more about protecting your privacy? The following organisations can offer free advice and resources to help you. 

The OAIC

The Office of the Australian Information Commissioner is the independent regulator for privacy and freedom of information, and provides guidance on how to protect your personal information.
IDCARE

IDCARE, Australia and New Zealand’s national identity and cyber support service, can help if you have concerns about identity fraud.

 
Scamwatch

Run by the Australian Competition and Consumer Commission, Scamwatch provides information, tools and resources to help you recognise, avoid and report scams.
