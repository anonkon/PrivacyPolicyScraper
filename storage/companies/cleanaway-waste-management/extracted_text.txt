Thank you for taking the time to visit our site. We appreciate your interest in our products and services. We understand you may have concerns about how your information is gathered when you visit our site and we want you to feel comfortable with the way we handle that information. Read our privacy policy here.
Our web server records information about your visit, such as length of your visit, the areas you visited and what type of information you viewed. This helps us improve our site’s content and performance by determining what areas our customers utilise the least/most. We may also use ‘cookies’ to track our visitor’s preferences. A cookie is a small file that is kept on your computer by the website and is read by the website at certain times to determine preferences such as how information is to be displayed while you’re using our site. Further personal data is only gathered if you willingly participate in surveys, contests, etc.
Cleanaway uses the information we gather about you from our website for the sole purpose of technical administration of its websites, improving our services and products, product surveys and marketing, and only to the extent necessary in each specific case. Except where it is required in the completion of our services or specifically approved by you, we will not provide any of the information about you that we have collected or stored to other companies, marketers, magazines or any other third parties.
