Experience the Essence of Luxury Living with Adairs
Welcome home, welcome to Adairs - Australia's leading name in premium home furnishings and décor. For more than two decades, we've been at the forefront of transforming spaces into homes filled with comfort, luxury, and timeless style. Every product we offer is a testament to our commitment to quality, design, and the sheer joy of home living.
Bedroom Bliss
Your bedroom is more than just a place to sleep; it's a sanctuary, a retreat from the hustle and bustle of daily life. At Adairs, our
bedroom range
is meticulously curated to ensure that you get the rest you deserve, night after night. Every item promises unparalleled comfort, from the silken touch of our high-thread-count sheets to the plush warmth of our quilts. Our range of beds, bedheads, and bedside tables are not just functional; they are statement pieces, each crafted to add a touch of elegance to your personal space. Add storage and comfort to your room, and make it look good in the process.
Bathroom Luxury
A crucial part of your routine, the bathroom is where you start and end your day. Our
bathroom collection
ensures those moments are wrapped in luxury. Experience the plush embrace of our towels, crafted from the finest materials to give you that spa-like feel right at home. Our range of bathroom accessories, from soap dispensers to storage solutions, is designed to add both style and functionality to your space.
Homewares Galore
Transform every bare wall and corner of your home with our exquisite
homewares
. From vibrant cushions that add a splash of colour to your living room to elegant throws that are perfect for those cosy nights in, our collection promises both style and comfort. Update your console tables, your bookshelves and more with a little finishing touch of style.
Kids Dreamland
Children's rooms are where dreams take flight. Our
Adairs Kids Range
is designed to help you create that perfect zone for playing, growing and dreaming. From whimsical bedding sets that transport your little ones to magical lands to furniture pieces that are both fun and functional, we make sure that your child's room is a space of joy and imagination. With cute kids bedroom themes like truck bedrooms, rainbow bedrooms, and unicorn bedrooms, there’s sure to be a good pick.
Gifts to Celebrate Every Occasion
Finding the perfect gift can be a challenge, but not with Adairs. Our curated
gift collection
offers something for everyone and every occasion. Whether it's a housewarming, a birthday, or just because, our range of gifts promises to delight and impress.

Furniture Craftsmanship.
Every piece of furniture tells a story, and at Adairs is ready to narrate your story with impeccable craftsmanship, innovative design, and timeless style. Our
furniture range
spans a range of pieces for every room in your home. Whether you're looking for a statement sofa for your living room, a functional desk for your study, or a chic bedside table for your bedroom, we have you covered.
Why Adairs?
We’re obsessed with helping you create the space of your dreams.
For all makeovers, big or small, our in-house designed range, artist collaborations, and friendly team are here to support your vision of home.
With more than 100 years of home behind us, we have the expert advice you need - whether it’s your place to escape, entertain, learn, or relax.
Proudly Australian owned, with our Linen Lovers program to give you early access to the best deals, it’s never been easier to style your space. Join us on this journey of luxury, comfort, and style. Dive deep into our range of products to discover the latest trends via our exclusive Adairs Insider guides and let us help you create a home that truly reflects who you are.
