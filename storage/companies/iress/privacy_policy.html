Award-winning technology

Iress FIX Hub has been named Best Cloud Provider to the Buy-Side at the 2025 WatersTechnology Buy-Side Technology Awards.

Designed for global trading firms, Iress FIX Hub (IFH) is an intelligent, self-service connectivity platform that empowers clients with visibility and control. It replaces complex, point-to-point networks with a single, cloud-native hub. Reducing cost, accelerating onboarding to a single day, and streamlining operations.

With secure global access, advanced mapping, and self-service tools, IFH helps buy-side firms connect faster, trade smarter, and stay in control.
